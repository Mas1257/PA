# ADR-0000: Architecture Decision Record Template

## Status

Template. Not a decision.

## Purpose

This template defines the format for all Architecture Decision Records in this
project. Copy it to create a new ADR.

Each ADR captures why a decision was made, not just what the decision was.
Six months from now, the reasoning behind a structural choice is easily lost.
The ADR preserves it.

## Format

Every ADR has these sections:

**Status** — Proposed, Accepted, Superseded, or Deprecated. If superseded, link
to the ADR that replaces it.

**Context** — The situation that forced a decision. What problem existed, what
constraints applied, what tensions had to be resolved.

**Decision** — What was decided, stated plainly.

**Consequences** — What this decision makes easy, what it makes hard, and what
it rules out. Both positive and negative.

**Alternatives Considered** — Other options and why they were not chosen.

## Numbering

ADRs are numbered sequentially: ADR-0001, ADR-0002, and so on. Numbers are never
reused. A superseded ADR keeps its number and status changes to Superseded.
